About Us

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) is a powerful security mechanism that restricts system access based on user roles. It enables organizations to manage and enforce policies around who can access specific resources and what actions they can perform. By assigning users to predefined roles, RBAC ensures that only authorized individuals have access to sensitive information, improving data security and minimizing the risk of unauthorized access.
